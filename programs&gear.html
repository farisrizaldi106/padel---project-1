Our Programs

Our padel programs are for all skill levels. Improve your game, have fun, and
join our community with our lessons and tournaments.

Program for Beginners: "The Padel Starter Package"

Invite them to start their padel journey in an easy and fun way. This package
is designed to eliminate any hesitation and provide an unforgettable first
experience.

What's Included

  • One Coaching Session (45 minutes)

    They'll learn the basics like how to hold a racket, simple serves, and
    fundamental rules.

  • Court & Equipment Rental (90 minutes)

    After the coaching, they can immediately play with friends using the
    rackets and balls we provide.

Program for Experienced Players: "Padel Academy"

Offer a structured training program focused on improving technique and
strategy. They will be trained by expert coaches to master specific shots like
the bandeja, the smash, and tactics for playing off the walls.

What's Included

  • Monthly Training Package

    4-8 intensive training sessions with a coach.

  • Video Analysis

    Recorded game sessions for in-depth analysis.

  • Physical Training Program

    Advice on off-court physical exercises to improve stamina and agility.

Program for the Community: "Padel Social Night"

Invite players of all levels to come and play in a relaxed and friendly atmosphere. They don't need to come with a partner; we'll arrange mix-and-match games so they can meet and play with new people.

What's Included

  • Mix-and-Match Format

    Every 20 minutes, partners are shuffled.

  • Complimentary Drinks & Snacks

    Add a nice touch by providing refreshments.

  • Music & Fun Atmosphere

    Create a lively and enjoyable environment.
